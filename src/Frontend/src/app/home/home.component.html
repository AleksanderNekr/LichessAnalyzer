About LichessAnalyzer

LichessAnalyzer is a free web-site for analyzing and visualizing Lichess.org™ players stats.

Abilities

Here you can create some analytics lists, add players from Lichess.org™ to them and view different graphs representing their stats in dynamic dashboards!

Contact & Rate us

Feel free to leave us a comment on the LichessAnalyzer GitHub™ page to give some feedback about this App!
